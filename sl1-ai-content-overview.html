Organizations producing content at the intersection of health, science, advocacy, or emerging industries face a specific problem: platforms suppress or penalize their content regardless of accuracy — while generic AI tools compound the risk by producing output that looks credible but creates real exposure. The AI Content System is built specifically for organizations where what you say publicly carries professional, regulatory, or platform-level consequences.

The problems this solves

Platform suppression
Instagram, YouTube, and Substack systematically throttle content in health, advocacy, cannabis, psychedelics, and wellness — regardless of scientific accuracy or journalistic quality. The system includes suppression monitoring and compliance-aware content architecture designed to survive platform review.
Voice consistency at scale
Generic AI produces generic output. Organizations with distinct authority and voice — clinicians, legal experts, academic researchers — lose that credibility the moment AI touches their content. A voice profile captures the specific language, framing, and claims standards before any content is produced.
Compliance claims
LegitScript certification, FTC claims discipline, platform community standards — organizations facing platform risk need content that works within these constraints without watering down the substance. The system is built around those constraints from the start, not added as a filter afterward.
Production bottleneck
Mission-driven organizations and small teams produce content far below the volume their authority and audience warrant. The system produces compliant, on-voice content at scale — without requiring the expert to write every word.
