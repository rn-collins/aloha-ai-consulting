The exposure

An ungoverned twin is a liability you carry

The market is long on realism and short on trust. The tools that clone your voice, face, and outbound have no answer for the rules that govern them. That gap lands on the person who deployed the twin.

FTC Fake Reviews Rule (16 CFR 465)

Generating fabricated or experience-faking reviews and testimonials carries penalties reported near $53k per violation. A content twin that invents social proof walks straight into it.

Likeness law: ELVIS Act, NO FAKES, right of publicity

Cloning a voice or likeness without written, use-specific consent implicates CA §3344.1 / AB 1836, the TN ELVIS Act, and NY §50-f. The NO FAKES Act has been advancing in Congress.

EU AI Act, Article 50

AI interaction and synthetic media carry disclosure and machine-readable marking duties. An undisclosed twin is a compliance gap the moment it speaks to an EU-touching audience.

Platform terms of service

The LinkedIn User Agreement and CAN-SPAM govern how a twin may reach anyone. An autopilot twin puts your primary account and your sender reputation at risk.

The difference

Governed generation versus the volume tools

Same generation quality on both sides. What changes is whether the output can stand in front of a general counsel, a regulator, or a platform trust team.

Ungoverned An off-the-shelf clone tool

  • Voice and likeness cloned with no signed, use-scoped consent on file.
  • No provenance credential, so the platform label cannot vouch for the asset.
  • Disclosure left to the operator to remember, or skipped entirely.
  • Health, efficacy, and capability claims ship unverified.
  • No red-team pass, so prompt-injection and impersonation go untested.
  • No human gate, so a bad output reaches an audience before anyone reviews it.

Governed A Trust-Safe Twin

  • Signed, use-scoped voice and likeness consent, with rights-expiry tracking.
  • C2PA Content Credentials engineered to survive to the platform label.
  • Automatic AI and automated-interaction disclosure, on by default.
  • Every claim gated against an evidence file before it can ship.
  • Red-team suite run on a schedule, with results logged for audit.
  • A human review gate on outputs that carry legal or reputational weight.

Proof, not slogan

The evidence gates a twin clears before handoff

Trust-safe is a set of numbers you can put in front of a general counsel. Every Built Twin clears these gates against the client's own human baseline before it goes live.

Voice ABX

Blind ABX discrimination at or below about 55%, with a persona human panel scoring 4/5 or better.

RAGAS faithfulness

Faithfulness at 0.90 or higher. A fabricated price or claim is a hard fail.

Injection-catch

95% or higher on a red-team suite covering direct, indirect, payload-splitting, and obfuscated attacks.

Human baseline

Reply, booking, or conversion that matches or beats your historical human numbers at equal-or-better safety.
