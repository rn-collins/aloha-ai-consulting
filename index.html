The problem

Serious organizations hit the same two walls with AI

They can't trust the facts

Generic AI hallucinates worst on the exact things that matter: the citation, the quota, the statutory trigger. I call it the Operator Execution Gap, the distance between what AI says and what you can act on. Closing it is the whole job.

Their people won't use it

A rollout stalls not because the tool is bad, but because no one built the trust and habit around it. Adoption is a neuroscience problem, not a software one, and that is exactly where my background sits.
