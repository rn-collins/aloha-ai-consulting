Legal AI Workflow

Legal AI that's fast, without ever becoming a sanctions risk.

Generic AI hallucinates worst on exactly what a legal team can't get wrong: the citation, the holding, the procedural rule. The answer is to build verification into the workflow, so speed never outruns accuracy, rather than banning AI or trusting it blindly. I design verification-gated AI workflows for law firms and legal teams on any platform, drawing on hands-on work inside an Am Law firm. For regulated or reputation-sensitive professions.

What this covers

Workflow design, plus advisory for firms and founders

This pillar spans the hands-on build and the guidance around it, on any platform your firm already uses, no rip-and-replace.

Verification-gated workflow design

The core build: AI workflows with verification checkpoints designed in, so every AI-assisted output is checked against source before it leaves the building. Scored for hallucination risk and platform-agnostic, so it works with whatever your firm already runs.

Firm & GC advisory

Guidance for law firms and general counsel adopting AI: where it's safe to accelerate, where a human gate is non-negotiable, and how to roll it out so associates and staff actually use it, without creating exposure.

Legal-tech founder advisory

For the people building the tools: product and workflow advisory that bakes verification and trust into legal-AI products from the design stage, not as an afterthought.

Adoption that sticks

A legal-AI rollout fails on trust, not features. The same neuroscience-of-adoption lens that runs through the whole practice makes sure the workflow is used, not quietly worked around.
