Framework: Nervous-System-Aware Platform-Risk Intelligence

The audience you lost was erased before anyone noticed

A removal is loud: a strike, an email, an appeal button. Reduced distribution is silent. The post stays up. The follower count holds. The dashboard looks calm. And the thousands of new people who would have found this month's content in their recommendations simply never arrive.

By the time the drop is obvious, you have often bled a full quarter of top-of-funnel growth, and the evidence you would need to appeal has gone cold. This is the most expensive event a regulated brand faces, and it is the one almost nobody is watching for.

Health, cannabis, and psychedelic content lives permanently inside the categories platforms commit, in their own policy text, to recommend less. YouTube "reduces recommendations of borderline content." TikTok publishes a named list of what it makes "ineligible for the For You feed," and Regulated Goods and Behavioral Health are on it. They admit they do it. They will not confirm when they have done it to you.
