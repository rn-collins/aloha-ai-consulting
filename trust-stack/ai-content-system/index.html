The cost of scaling content you have not grounded

You already know AI can write a week of posts in an afternoon. In a regulated shop, every one of those posts is a potential compliance artifact: logged, supervised, and read back to you later by someone whose job is to find the sentence that should not have been published.

A single ungrounded claim is a correction, a finding, or a fine. Automate the generation of that claim without grounding it and you have not removed the risk. You have multiplied it by your posting volume. Every channel. Every week. On a schedule.

Two costs compound at once. Hallucination risk: models reliably produce statements no source can verify, and it scales with your automation. Brand risk: ungrounded content fails the reader's source-credibility check and off-voice content fails the fluency check, because inconsistent writing is judged as less true. Faster is not the goal. Trusted-and-fast is.
