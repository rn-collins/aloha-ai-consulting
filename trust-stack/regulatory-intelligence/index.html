Framework: The IDR Engine (Ingest → Digest → Regurgitate)

The expensive failure this prevents

A lawyer asked a general-purpose model what the law required. It answered fluently, with case names and internal quotations. The lawyer filed it. The cases did not exist.

In Mata v. Avianca (S.D.N.Y. 2023) a federal judge imposed a $5,000 Rule 11 sanction after a brief cited judicial decisions the model had invented. In Wadsworth v. Walmart (D. Wyo. 2025), eight of nine cited cases were invented and the court revoked one attorney's pro hac vice admission. As of mid-2026, one public database (maintained by researcher Damien Charlotin) catalogued more than 1,590 cases worldwide in which AI-fabricated authorities were identified in filings, a count rising by roughly ten a day; re-verify the live figure before citing it.

Every one was the same failure: a fact about what the law requires traveled forward without being checked against a primary source. The polish of the output was mistaken for its truth. A fabricated citation is a trust rupture. The moment a reader pulls one cite and it does not resolve, the credibility of your entire record collapses.

What's inside

  • The IDR Engine — Ingest, Digest, Regurgitate. Ingest ranks every source by legal weight before it enters. Digest verifies, structures, and scores it. Regurgitate emits only claims that survive a decision-maker's credibility check.
  • The neuroscience of why sourced facts get acted on. Appraisal theory, predictive processing, and source-credibility research, cited to their venues.
  • The authority hierarchy. Primary versus secondary, mandatory versus persuasive, mapped onto the APA rulemaking chain (5 U.S.C. § 553).
  • Two worked failures. Mata and Wadsworth, dissected with verified sanction figures and the exact point each broke.
  • A production-ready JSON record schema with a filled example, so every claim carries its pinpoint citation, retrieval date, confidence, and change-watch trigger.
  • A live maintenance protocol tied to the Federal Register, eCFR, and GovInfo APIs, plus the IDR verification checklist: every gate a claim must clear before it leaves Layer 0.
