The rule

Use the right tool for the ordinary parts. Own the part that's the invention.

Most projects are a mix. Some of what you need is completely ordinary — registration, checkout, a contact list, confirmation emails. Some of it is the actual thing that makes your business yours. The mistake is treating both the same: either hand-building the boring parts from scratch, or renting a template that owns your custom work forever. This service sorts the two and wires each to the right place.

Ordinary — let a proven backend handle it

  • Event and workshop registration
  • Payments, coupons, and checkout
  • Bookings and scheduling
  • Contacts and customer records
  • Confirmation and marketing email
  • Product catalogs and memberships

The invention — you own it outright

  • Your custom-designed frontend
  • Your source code, in your own GitHub
  • Your brand, layout, and content
  • Portability — move hosts anytime
  • Room to add custom features later
  • No lock-in to anyone's template

The stack

Three pieces, each doing what it's best at

The point isn't the tools — it's putting each job where it belongs, so you get a custom site you own without maintaining infrastructure you shouldn't have to.

GitHub

Your code, your IP

The site's source lives in your own repository with full version history. It's yours, inspectable, and portable.

Vercel

Hosting & deploys

Builds and publishes the site automatically, with preview versions to check changes before they go live. Fast CDN, SSL included.

Headless backend

The business functions

Registration, payments, bookings, contacts, and email handled by a proven system, connected to your custom frontend — not rebuilt from scratch.

You walk away owning the repo and the design. That's the difference from renting a template site you can never take with you.
