What your score means
0–1 YES
Foundation first. Your firm is pre-AI-workflow. The first build is governance: a usage policy, an approved tool stack, and a basic hallucination review protocol. Everything else sits on top of that.
2–3 YES
Ready to build. You have awareness without infrastructure. The right move is a workflow audit: map the three highest-volume non-billable tasks and design one AI-assisted prompt sequence for each — using whichever platform fits your firm's stack. Start narrow, prove value, expand.
4–5 YES
Ready to scale. You have the foundation. The opportunity is systematizing what's already working — prompt libraries, document automation templates, and staff training that makes the system consistent across the whole team, not just the one attorney who figured it out.
