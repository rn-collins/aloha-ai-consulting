The Agent Role Contract

A named AI agent is cute. A governed one is trustworthy — and the difference is one page. This is the contract that sits under every agent Aloha AI builds: what it may do, what it may never do, and what it must route to a human. Fill it in below and download your copy. Free, runs in your browser, sends nothing anywhere.

Fill it in

One agent, sixteen fields

Write it for any agent you will actually rely on. A quick tip: if you can't fill in Prohibited actions and Human approval, the agent isn't ready to be trusted with anything consequential.

The recognizable name (optional — the contract, not the name, is the point).
The one job this agent holds.
The business outcome it serves.
The tasks it may perform.
The information it may receive.
How sensitive that information is.
The systems it may access.
What it may never do. Be specific.
The shape of what it returns.
What sources or support it must show.
When it must say it is unsure.
Which actions require your sign-off.
Conditions that force a human review.
What gets recorded.
How long records are kept.
How you will judge its quality.
What happens when it breaks.
The human accountable — always a person.

Nothing you type is sent anywhere — the download is generated in your browser.
