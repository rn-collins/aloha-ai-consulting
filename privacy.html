Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026  ·  Governing law: State of Hawaii

Operator

This website, aloha-ai-consulting.vercel.app (the “Site”), is operated by RN Collins LLC (“Aloha AI Consulting,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of Hawaii, doing business as Aloha AI Consulting. For privacy inquiries, contact us at collins.ra@husky.neu.edu.

What We Collect

We collect two categories of information:

  • Email addresses and contact information submitted voluntarily through the contact form or project scope form on this Site. This may also include your name and a description of your project or inquiry, if you choose to provide them.
  • Usage data collected automatically by Vercel Analytics and Vercel Speed Insights when you visit the Site. This includes page views, referral sources, browser type, device type, and general geographic region (country/region level). Vercel processes this data on our behalf as described in Vercel’s Privacy Policy.

We do not collect payment information directly. Any purchases or transactions are processed through third-party platforms (e.g., Gumroad) under their own privacy policies.

How We Use Your Information

Information you submit through the contact or scope forms is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or assess your project scope
  • Follow up on consulting engagement discussions
  • Communicate project updates if an engagement is initiated

Usage data collected via Vercel Analytics is used to understand how the Site is being used, identify technical issues, and improve the Site’s content and performance. We do not use usage data to build individual user profiles or deliver targeted advertising.

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share data only as follows:

  • Vercel, Inc. — as our hosting and analytics provider. Vercel processes usage data and hosts this Site. See Vercel’s Privacy Policy.
  • Legal compliance — if required by applicable law, regulation, or valid legal process, we may disclose information as required.

No other third parties receive your personal information.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions (email addresses, names, and messages) are retained for up to 12 months from the date of submission. After that period, submissions are deleted unless an active consulting engagement requires continued retention.

Usage analytics data is retained per Vercel’s standard data retention policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information you have submitted to us through this Site. To exercise any of these rights, email collins.ra@husky.neu.edu with your request. We will respond within a reasonable time and no later than 30 days.

Cookies and Analytics

This Site uses Vercel Analytics, which may set minimal analytics cookies or use local storage to measure site performance and visitor behavior. These are analytics-only cookies. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or cross-site tracking technology.

If you prefer not to be tracked by Vercel Analytics, you may disable JavaScript in your browser or use a browser extension that blocks analytics scripts.

Children’s Privacy

This Site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us at the address below and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page reflects when the most recent changes were made. Continued use of the Site after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
