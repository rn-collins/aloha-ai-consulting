Privacy

The honest, short version.

This site doesn't track you. No cookies, no analytics, no pixels. The interactive tools run entirely in your browser and send nothing anywhere. The only information I ever hold is what you choose to send me, by booking a call or by email. That's the whole policy; the rest is detail.

Privacy policy

Effective 2026-07-04 · Aloha AI, a DBA of Rayven-Nikkita Collins LLC · Honolulu, HI

No tracking, no cookies, no analytics

This website sets no tracking cookies and runs no analytics or advertising scripts. I don't build a profile of you, and there are no third-party trackers loading in the background. Your visit is your business.

The tools run in your browser

The interactive tools, monitors, and figures on this site compute entirely on your own device. When you enter numbers or answers into a tool, that input stays in your browser. It is not transmitted to me, to a server, or to any third party. Close the tab and it's gone.

What is collected

The only personal information I hold is what you voluntarily provide when you get in touch:

  • If you book a call: the details you enter in the booking form (typically your name, email address, and any note you add), handled through Microsoft Outlook Bookings.
  • If you email me: your email address, your name if you include it, and whatever you write in the message.

That's it. No account is required to use anything on this site.

How it's used

I use the details you send for one purpose: to respond to you and, if we work together, to run the engagement. I don't add you to a marketing list, and I don't message you about anything you didn't ask about.

Storage & third parties

Because the collection is so limited, so is the exposure:

  • Bookings are processed by Microsoft (Outlook Bookings). Details you enter are handled under Microsoft's terms and privacy practices.
  • Email lives in my ordinary mail client and reaches me through your own mail provider. No separate email platform or CRM sits in between.

These providers act as the plumbing for the message; I don't share your information with anyone beyond what's needed to receive and reply to you.

No sale of data

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Ever. There's no data business here to sell it to.

Your rights

You can ask me what information I hold about you, and you can ask me to delete it. Since the only data I have is what you sent, honoring either request is simple. Just email me (below) and I'll take care of it.

Children

This site is intended for professionals and is not directed to children under 13. I don't knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has sent me information, email me and I'll delete it.

Changes

If this policy changes, the effective date above will change with it. Given the "collect almost nothing" approach, material changes are unlikely.

Governing law

This policy is governed by the laws of the State of Hawaii.

Questions?

Ask what I hold, or ask me to delete it.
