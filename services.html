AI teams for business

AI teams you can actually trust with real work

We map how your business works, build a supervised team of AI agents around it, and create the controls, evidence, and operating procedures needed to use that team responsibly.

The idea

Not an automation. A team.

Businesses think in people and responsibilities, a researcher, an analyst, a compliance reviewer, a chief of staff, not prompts and webhooks. Aloha AI builds AI workforces with clear roles, controlled access, human approvals, and measurable outputs. Every agent runs on a governed role contract underneath the friendly name.

The service ladder

Four levels, from map to governed system

Level 1 · Discovery

AI Team Discovery

We examine the business and find the work AI should, and should not, touch.

  • Workflow map
  • AI-readiness assessment
  • Agent-opportunity register
  • Risk classification
  • Recommended team architecture
  • Implementation roadmap
Level 2 · Prototype

AI Team Prototype

A controlled demonstration on realistic but non-sensitive data.

  • Working prototype
  • Agent role cards
  • Prompt and instruction architecture
  • Handoff logic
  • Sample outputs and known limits
  • Human-approval map
Level 3 · Deployment

AI Team Deployment

Integrated with your real systems: email, Drive, Slack, CRM, calendar, databases.

  • Production workflow
  • Access-control design
  • Logging and monitoring
  • Evaluation suite
  • SOPs and staff training
  • Maintenance plan
Level 4 · Governed OS · The moat

Governed AI Operating System

The premium layer most agent builders do not offer: a system you can prove is safe.

  • Agent, model, and data-source registries
  • Permission matrix
  • Human-approval rules
  • Audit trail and incident response
  • Output-evaluation framework
  • Records, retention, and vendor-risk
  • Change management
  • Quarterly system audit

This is the move from "I built some agents" to architecting governed AI work systems for organizations.
