The Operator Execution Gap · live

Ask a generic AI to cite-check a holding. Watch it invent a case.

Large language models fail most confidently exactly where a lawyer's exposure is highest: the citation. Same request, two answers: the fluent-but-fabricated generic reply, and the verified, source-checked one. The distance between them is a Rule 11 problem.

✕ Generic LLM · unverified

"Give me a controlling case for the proposition that a forum-selection clause survives novation, with a pin cite."

"Certainly. The leading authority is:"

Hartwell v. Meridian Logistics, Inc., 612 F.3d 448, 456 (9th Cir. 2011) — "a valid forum-selection clause is presumed to survive novation absent express extinguishment."

Reads perfectly. Correct reporter format, plausible circuit, confident pin cite. The case does not exist. Neither the parties, the volume, nor the holding are real. The model assembled a citation shaped like the answer you wanted.

✓ Aloha AI workflow · source-verified Checkpoint: retrieval + reporter check

Same request, run through a verification-first workflow:

No citation is returned until it resolves against a real reporter. The workflow retrieves candidate authority from a citator/database, confirms the case exists, that it stands for the stated proposition, and that it remains good law, then surfaces the pin cite with its verification trail.

STATUS: no verified controlling authority located for this exact proposition in the requested circuit. Escalate to human research. Do not draft around an unconfirmed cite.

Confidence: High  Why you can rely on it: the workflow is built to return "not found" rather than fabricate. An honest null beats a confident invention every time in a filing.

How it works · workflow design

Three stages, with the human on the load-bearing one

A trustworthy legal-AI workflow isn't "use a better model." It's a designed pipeline: intake, AI drafting bounded by checkpoints, and human verification before anything is certified.

Intake & task framing (RIL)

Scope the task, the jurisdiction, and the stakes up front. The Regulatory Intelligence Layer decides which parts a model may touch and grounds retrieval in real authority, not the model's memory.

AI drafting with checkpoints (ROL)

The model drafts inside guardrails: every citation must resolve against a citator, every factual claim carries a source, and the workflow is built to return "not found" instead of inventing. This is the Resolve-to-Operator Layer: output an attorney can actually work from.

Human verification & sign-off

A qualified human verifies the load-bearing steps (cites, holdings, privilege calls) before certification. Monitors re-check their sources on a schedule (Upstash · Vercel · Exa) so citators and rule changes stay current rather than freezing on build day.
